Few example types of Paint You'll produce in the training

Emulsion Paint

A water-based paint ideal for both interior and exterior walls, offering smooth finishing and easy application.

Textured Paint

Gives walls a decorative look with a rough or patterned surface, perfect for unique designs and accents.

Satin Paint

A semi-gloss paint with a soft sheen, suitable for areas like bedrooms and offices that need elegance.

Gloss Paint

A shiny, durable paint used for doors, windows, and wooden or metal surfaces to achieve a polished look.

Matte Paint

Known for its flat finish and ability to hide surface imperfections, perfect for living spaces and ceilings.

Oil-Based Paint

Durable and moisture-resistant, ideal for exterior surfaces or areas exposed to frequent contact.
